User data and privacy policy

StreamFusion stores data about you and your usage of the program locally on your computer. This data is not shared, in any form, with anyone else other than you, and StreamFusion does not transmit this information to anywhere outside your computer. This data includes:

  • What music you have added to your library
  • What music you have downloaded
  • What music you listen to
  • Monthly play counts per track
  • How many times you have listened to a track

StreamFusion does not explicitly transmit any collected user information to any third party. However, during normal operation of StreamFusion, third parties may be able to collect some information on you, acquired from the traffic between StreamFusion and third party service providers. This information may include, but may not be limited to:

  • What music you have downloaded
  • What music you listen to
  • What you search for
  • Your IP address

In addition to this, StreamFusion does inform an external internet server when you update StreamFusion. During this process, only information about the version you are updating to, and the version you are updating from are sent to the server. This information is not shared with anyone in an identifiable manner.
